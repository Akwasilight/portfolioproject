About comapany

Bellabeat is a technology company founded in 2013 that aims to create innovative health and wellness products specifically designed for women. The company is committed to empowering women with tools and knowledge to help them achieve their wellness goals and improve their overall quality of life. Bellabeats products, including smart jewelry and fitness trackers, are designed to track various aspects of womens health, such as activity levels, sleep quality, stress, and reproductive health. The companys products combine technology and aesthetics, with a focus on creating devices that are both functional and stylish.

Buiness task

Analyze smart device usage data in order to gain insight into how people are already using their smart devices. Then, give recommendations for how these trends can inform Bellabeat marketing strategy

source of dataset

The data set generated by respondents to a distributed survey via Amazon Mechanical Turk, public data that explores smart device users daily habits FitBit Fitness Tracker Data (CC0: Public Domain, data set made available through Mobius): This Kaggle data set contains personal fitness tracker from thirty fitbit users. Thirty eligible Fitbit users consented to the submission of personal tracker data, including minute-level output for physical activity, heart rate, and sleep monitoring.

1.What are some trends in smart device usage? 2 . How could these trends apply to Bella beat customers? 3. How could these trends help influence Bella beat marketing strategy?

Conclusion and Recommendation

1.What are some trends in smart device usage?

The results from the data shows that there is a positive relationship in High very active minutes of users, total distance covered, and total steps to calories burnt. Therefore, a user who is very active for more minutes will have increase in calories burnt and also any user covering more distance and taken more steps daily will also have an increase in calories burnt recorded by the device. Daily habit of spending more time in bed result indicate that a user will have less very active minutes, and also may not or will burn less calories during the day, and will also have fewer total steps count by the device. More count on total steps helps in covering more distance.

  1. How could these trends apply to Bellabeat customers?

Bellabeat can ensure their customers on having more very active minutes or having more total steps count by their product will result in burning more calories, and for customers who want to burn more calories can try having more walk and high very active minutes count by their device.

3 how these trends can inform Bellabeat marketing strategy ?

Bellabeat company can set a notification system on their device to inform user on if they have lose or gain more calories use the trends and also predict on their daily toatal step, very active minute and total distance using their total time spend in bed.
